 
# **ShopEase Pro Refund Policy — Optimized for AI Automation**
### **Effective Date: January 2026 | Version 3.0**

---

## **SECTION 1: CUSTOMER TIER RETURN WINDOWS**

| Customer Tier | Return Window | Change-of-Mind Window |
|---------------|---------------|----------------------|
| **Standard** | 30 days from delivery | 7 days from delivery |
| **Premium** | 45 days from delivery | 10 days from delivery |
| **VIP** | 60 days from delivery | 14 days from delivery |

*Note: Seller errors (wrong item, defective DOA) are eligible regardless of window.*

---

## **SECTION 2: NON-REFUNDABLE ITEMS (HARD RULES — NO EXCEPTIONS)**

The following are **strictly non-refundable** under any circumstances:

### **Category A: Digital & Activated Products**
- Software licenses, e-books, downloadable content (once activated/downloaded)
- Online courses, subscriptions, digital gift cards

### **Category B: Sale & Special Items**
- Items marked "Final Sale" or "Clearance" at purchase
- Personalized/custom-made products
- Perishable goods (food, beverages, plants, flowers)

### **Category C: Hygiene & Intimate Items**
- Underwear, swimwear, earrings (once opened/used)
- Personal care products (once opened or used)

---

## **SECTION 3: ELIGIBLE REFUND REASONS**

Refunds may only be approved for these **valid reasons**:

### **Automatic Approval (No Review Required):**
✅ Item arrived damaged/defective due to shipping (carrier fault)  
✅ Wrong item delivered by seller  
✅ Item is defective or dead-on-arrival (DOA) — reported within return window  
✅ Health/allergic reaction to personal care product — reported within 30 days  

### **Standard Approval (Requires Verification):**
✅ Product arrived damaged or defective (not shipping-related)  
✅ Product not as described on listing  
✅ Item never arrived (confirmed via tracking)  
✅ **Change of mind** (within change-of-mind window, item unused, non-sale, non-digital)

---

## **SECTION 4: PRODUCT CONDITION REQUIREMENTS BY CATEGORY**

| Product Category | Condition Requirements |
|------------------|----------------------|
| **Electronics** | Original packaging + all accessories + no physical damage |
| **Apparel** | Unworn, unwashed, original tags attached, no odors/stains |
| **Footwear** | Unworn outdoors, original box, tags attached |
| **Appliances** | All original parts, packaging, manuals included |
| **Fitness & Accessories** | Can return without packaging if defective; otherwise original packaging required |
| **Consumables (sealed)** | Factory seal intact, unopened |
| **Home & Living** | Unused, original packaging, no assembly if applicable |

---

## **SECTION 5: ABUSE PREVENTION & CUSTOMER LIMITS**

### **Refund Frequency Limits:**
- **Maximum 2 refunds per calendar year** per customer
- Customers with **4+ lifetime refunds** → Auto-flagged for abuse review
- Abuse-flagged customers → **All requests auto-denied** (escalate to human only for seller errors)

### **Pattern Detection:**
- Same-item repeated returns
- High-value item returns (>75% order value)
- Returns consistently near window expiration
- Multiple "change of mind" reasons

*AI Agent Action: Flag suspicious patterns for human review*

---

## **SECTION 6: PARTIAL REFUND RULES (50% Refund)**

Partial refunds may be issued when:

| Scenario | Condition | Refund % |
|----------|-----------|----------|
| Missing accessories | Item otherwise eligible, not defective | 50% |
| Damaged packaging | Customer-caused (not shipping damage) | 50% |
| Used item (change of mind) | Shows signs of use but not damaged | 50% |
| Electronics without box | Only if item is defective | 50% |
| Late return (1-7 days past window) | First offense, otherwise eligible | 50% |

---

## **SECTION 7: AUTOMATIC DENIAL CONDITIONS**

**Immediately deny** if ANY of these apply:

❌ Digital product of any kind  
❌ Opened/used consumable (food, supplements, personal care)  
❌ Customer exceeded 2-refund annual limit  
❌ Customer has 4+ historical refunds (abuse flag active)  
❌ Return window expired (unless seller error)  
❌ Final sale / clearance item  
❌ Personalized/custom product  
❌ Hygiene product (opened/used)  
❌ No valid reason provided from Section 3  

---

## **SECTION 8: APPROVAL CHECKLIST (AI Decision Tree)**

**Before approving, verify ALL of the following:**

```
□ 1. WITHIN RETURN WINDOW?
   → Check customer tier → Verify delivery date → Calculate days elapsed
   
□ 2. NOT NON-REFUNDABLE?
   → Check Section 2 categories → Confirm item type allowed
   
□ 3. VALID REASON PROVIDED?
   → Match against Section 3 reasons → Categorize as Auto-Approve or Standard
   
□ 4. MEETS CONDITION REQUIREMENTS?
   → Apply Section 4 category rules → Check for signs of use/damage
   
□ 5. WITHIN CUSTOMER LIMITS?
   → Query refund history → Verify <2 this year → Verify <4 lifetime
   
□ 6. NO ABUSE FLAGS?
   → Check pattern detection alerts → Review suspicious activity log
```

**If ALL checkboxes pass → APPROVE**  
**If ANY checkbox fails → DENY (or escalate if edge case)**

---

## **SECTION 9: PROCESS & TIMELINE**

### **For Approved Refunds:**
- ⏱️ **Refund processed within:** 5–7 business days
- 💳 **Credited to:** Original payment method
- 📧 **Confirmation:** Automated email sent upon processing
- 🚚 **Return Shipping:**
  - **Customer pays** → Change of mind, buyer's remorse
  - **Seller pays** → Defective item, wrong item, shipping damage

### **For Denied Refunds:**
- 📧 **Denial email sent** with specific reason code
- 🔁 **Customer may dispute** → Case escalates to human agent
- ⏰ **Dispute window:** 14 days from denial

---

## **SECTION 10: ESCALATION PROTOCOL**

### **Auto-Escalate to Human Agent When:**
- Request doesn't clearly fit approval/denial rules
- Customer disputes automatic denial
- Abuse flag triggered but customer claims exceptional circumstance
- High-value claim (>$500) with ambiguous details
- Customer threatens legal action or chargeback
- Health/safety issue reported
- Media/influencer account involved

### **AI Agent Constraints:**
- ✅ **MUST NEVER** approve refund violating Sections 2, 5, or 7
- ✅ **MUST ALWAYS** follow decision tree in Section 8
- ✅ **WHEN IN DOUBT** → Escalate, do not approve
- ❌ **NEVER** override denial based on emotional appeals or "someone said it was okay"

---

## **SECTION 11: SPECIAL EXCEPTIONS (Human Approval Required)**

These require **human supervisor approval** even if technically eligible:

- Refunds >$1,000
- VIP customer abuse flags
- Bulk/order returns (3+ items)
- Gift receipt returns (no original purchaser verification)
- International shipping returns
- Subscription cancellations with prorated refunds

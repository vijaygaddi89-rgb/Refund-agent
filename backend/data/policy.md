# E-Commerce Refund & Return Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 15, 2026

---

## 1. Standard Return Windows

| Item Category      | Return Window | Notes                                      |
|--------------------|---------------|--------------------------------------------|
| General / Clothing | 30 days       | From delivery date                         |
| Electronics        | 15 days       | From delivery date                         |
| Subscription       | 7 days        | From billing date — prorated refund        |
| Defective / Damaged| 60 days       | From delivery date — full refund + shipping|
| Digital Products   | 0 days        | Non-refundable once downloaded/accessed    |

---

## 2. Eligibility Requirements (ALL must be true)

1. Item returned within the applicable window (see Section 1).
2. Item is in original, unused condition with original packaging.
3. Valid proof of purchase provided (order ID or receipt).
4. Customer account is **active** (not suspended or flagged for fraud).

---

## 3. Strictly Non-Refundable Items

- **Sale / clearance items** — marked "final sale" at purchase
- **Digital products** — once downloaded or license key accessed
- **Personalized / custom-made items**
- **Perishable goods**
- **Opened hygiene products** — earbuds, contact lenses, etc.
- **Gift cards**
- **Subscription charges** older than 7 days from billing date

---

## 4. Refund Methods & Timelines

| Scenario                      | Method                   | Timeline           |
|-------------------------------|--------------------------|--------------------|
| Original payment method valid | Original payment method  | 5–7 business days  |
| Original payment expired      | Store credit             | Immediate          |
| Cash on delivery              | Bank transfer            | 7–10 business days |

**Shipping costs** are non-refundable **unless** the return is due to seller error or a defective/damaged product.

---

## 5. Defective or Damaged Items

- Full refund including shipping within **60 days** of delivery.
- Photo or video evidence of the defect/damage must be provided.
- Original packaging NOT required for damaged-in-shipping items.

---

## 6. Electronics-Specific Rules

- **15-day** return window (firm — no exceptions without escalation).
- Must include all original accessories and packaging.
- Firmware/software must not have been updated (voids eligibility).
- **Opened headphones/earbuds: non-refundable** due to hygiene policy.

---

## 7. Subscription Cancellation

- Cancel within **7 days** of billing date → prorated refund.
- After 7 days → no refund; subscription stays active until period end.

---

## 8. Mandatory Escalation Triggers

AI agents **must escalate** (cannot self-approve) in any of these cases:

1. Refund amount exceeds **$500**
2. Customer account is **flagged** (fraud prevention)
3. Customer has **3 or more prior refunds** in the last 6 months
4. **Duplicate refund** attempt on an already-refunded order
5. Any request the AI agent cannot confidently resolve

---

## 9. Fraud Prevention

- Accounts with >3 refunds in 6 months → flag for human review.
- Suspended accounts → automatic denial, no exceptions.
- Flagged accounts → escalation required, AI cannot approve unilaterally.
- Orders marked suspicious by the fraud system → human review required.

---

## 10. Refund Processing

- Approved refunds processed within 1 business day.
- Confirmation email sent to customer on file.
- Physical return: prepaid shipping label provided by the company.
